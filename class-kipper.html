Why Kipper?

Kipper is the "Sister" of Lenormand, but with a focus on people and situations. While other decks might be abstract, Kipper is cinematic. It shows you the actors in your life the boss, the lover, the false person, the unexpected money. It is the most talkative and situational deck in existence.

In this class, you will learn to read Kipper cards as a narrative. You'll understand how cards "look" at each other, creating a dynamic map of your life's current path.
