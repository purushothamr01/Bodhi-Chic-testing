✦ My Story

A Healer Born from
Cosmic Wisdom

I am an eclectic neo-pagan witch, a born Hindu, an Andromedan, and a shamanic practitioner. My path is deeply rooted in the profound belief in healing and universal energies. What began as a personal quest for answers became a sacred mission to help others navigate life with clarity, courage, and deep self-trust.

I am a fortune teller and healer who helps you make better choices using Tarot as a guiding tool. I work with energy healing, Akashic Records, angelic guidance, and Access Consciousness to help you upgrade your life at every dimension physical, emotional, financial, and spiritual.

My work goes beyond predictions. It is about empowering you to become the conscious creator of your own destiny aligned, healed, and whole.
