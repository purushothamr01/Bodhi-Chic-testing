Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

At Uttara Studios, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you visit our website or book our services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when expressing an interest in obtaining information about us or our services, such as:

  • Name and Contact Data (e.g., email address, phone number).
  • Booking Information (e.g., dates, session preferences).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect or receive to:

  • Facilitate account creation and booking processes.
  • Send administrative information to you, such as appointment confirmations.
  • Respond to your inquiries and offer support.

3. Confidentiality of Sessions

All spiritual readings, healing sessions, and consultations (including Tarot, Akashic Records, and Reiki) are strictly confidential. We do not record or share the intimate details of your sessions with any third parties.

4. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services (like WhatsApp for communication and booking). Please note that these third-party services have their own privacy policies governing the use of your data.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions or comments about this policy, you may email us at connect@uttarastudios.com.
